Pandamian

I co-founded Pandamian in 2010, and worked on it as a designer and developer.

Pandamian's premise was that current ebook production workflows were really bad, and writers would be better served with a web-based tool for producing their ebooks. This turned out to be the wrong problem to solve (also my co-founders and I were new to the game). We've since shut down the company.

Startup woes aside, Pandamian's interface was the result of an obsessive focus on simplicity. These screenshots are from the third iteration of Pandamian's UI; while the app did not succeed, we did get multiple happy reviews from users and educators who had been using the product (for their personal books, and as a teaching aid).

Pandamian was built with Django and Python.
