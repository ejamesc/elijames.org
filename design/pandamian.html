Pandamian

I co-founded Pandamian in 2010, and served as its designer and developer.

The premise behind Pandamian was that current ebook production workflows were painful, and writers would be better served with a web-based tool for producing their books. This turned out to be the wrong problem to solve (and, well, my co-founders and I were new to the game). We've since shut down the company.

Startup woes aside, Pandamian's interface was the result of an obsessive focus on simplicity. You're looking at the third iteration of Pandamian's UI; while the app did not succeed, we did get multiple happy reviews from users and educators who had been using the product.

Pandamian was built with Django and Python.
