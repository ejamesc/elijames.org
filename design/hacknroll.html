Hack&Roll 2013

Hack&Roll is a 24 hour hackathon organized annually by the NUS Hackers. (I led the team that first created it in 2012, and hung around long enough to organize the 2013 round as well).

I was responsible for designing all the promotional and branding materials for Hack&Roll'13: posters, stickers, shirts and website.

Site built in Express.js. Poster icons taken from the noun project.
