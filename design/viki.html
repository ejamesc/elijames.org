Viki Developers

I did a software engineering internship at Viki in the summer of 2012,working on the Viki platform, amongst other things.

Viki was putting the finishing touches on version 3 of their API and needed proper documentation for their partners. I wrote the majority of the documentation on the side, and did a quick redesign of their developer's site in preparation for a few partner launches.

The site may be found at dev.viki.com.

Site built using Jekyll and Bootstrap, served using Github pages.
