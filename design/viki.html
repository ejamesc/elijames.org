Viki Developers

I did a software engineering internship at Viki in the summer of 2012, contributing, amongst other things, to the development of the Viki platform.

Viki was putting the finishing touches on version 3 of their API and needed proper documentation for their partners. I wrote the majority of the documentation on the side, and did a quick redesign of their entire developer's site in preparation of a few partner launches.

The site may be found at dev.viki.com. Site built using Jekyll and Bootstrap.
