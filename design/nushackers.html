NUS Hackers

I designed and managed most of NUS Hackers's web properties from 2011 to 2013, in addition to my duties as President.

Chief amongst this was nushackers.org, the primary web presence for the club. I kept visual styling to a minimum and focused on clear copywriting and content. We were trying to spread a fairly nuanced message (i.e. that hacking is good for Singapore!) so this website was necessary to establish and clarify that message.

This design has served us well over the course of 2 years, even as we've expanded our activities to include hackathons, workshops, and weekly talks.

nushackers.org runs on Wordpress and uses a custom theme, designed and built over a weekend.
