NUS Hackers

I designed and managed most of NUS Hackers's web properties from 2011-2013, in addition to my duties as President.

Chief amongst this was nushackers.org, the primary web presence for the club. I chose to keep visual styling to a minimum and focused on clear copywriting and content, as NUS Hackers (back then) was relatively unknown.

The theme has served us well over the course of 2 years, even as we've expanded our activities to include hackathons, workshops, and weekly talks.

nushackers.org runs on Wordpress and uses a custom theme, designed and built over a weekend.
