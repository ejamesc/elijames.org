Eli James is a nom-de-plume

My real name's Cedric Chin. I started using Eli as an alias because I began doing digital publishing work at a really young age. Using a nom-de-plume allowed my ideas to be evaluated independent of my background. I thought this was really cool.

Eventually, though, the name stuck when EJ became increasingly associated with my body of work.

Feel free to call me with whichever name you please - I respond comfortably to both.
