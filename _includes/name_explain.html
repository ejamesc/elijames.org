Eli James is a nom-de-plume

My real name's Cedric Chin. I used Eli as an alias because I started doing digital publishing work at a really young age. Using a nom-de-plume enabled my ideas to be evaluated independent of my background. This I found to be pretty cool.

Eventually, though, the name stuck when EJ became increasingly associated with my body of work.

Feel free to call me by whichever name you please - I respond to both.
